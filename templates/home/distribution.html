Tablet Distribution and Testing


Tablet Distribution

A total of 1,00,000 tablets were received by IIT Bombay from Datawind. These were distributed among various remote center colleges and schools across the country. For the details of remote-center wise receipts of the tablets visit Distribution Details of Tablet.
IIT Kharagpur, now a partner of IITB for Aakash related activities, has taken up the responsibility of new Remote Centers in the eastern and northern India and to distribute Aakash for testing and application development.


Testing

Lab Testing by C-DAC (Thiruvananthapuram)

CDAC Thiruvananthapuram was entrusted the responsibility of carrying out the detailed lab testing of the Aakash tablet samples as part of the Aakash project given by MHRD to IIT Bombay. Testing was done in five phases :

  • Aakash Status Report dated 22 August 2012
  • Aakash Status Report dated 30 May 2013
  • ERTL Test Report dated 15 April 2013
  • Aakash Status Report dated 8 July 2013
  • Aakash Test Report dated 8 July 2013
